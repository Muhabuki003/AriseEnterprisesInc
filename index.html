History

Arise Enterprises, Inc. has been in business for 38 years. We are located in West Houston and serve the Greater Houston Area with full-service electrical work.

To put the ideals of Arise Enterprises, Inc. into a short list: honesty, fairness, swiftness, and efficiency are some of the most important values we've held for the entire time we have been in business — all while keeping our work in line with the current National Electric Code (NEC).
